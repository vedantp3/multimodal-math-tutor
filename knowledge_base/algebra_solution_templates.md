# Algebra Solution Templates

## Template: Solving Quadratic Equations
**Step 1**: Write the equation in standard form: ax² + bx + c = 0
**Step 2**: Identify coefficients a, b, c
**Step 3**: Calculate discriminant D = b² - 4ac
**Step 4**: Apply the quadratic formula: x = (-b ± √D) / 2a
**Step 5**: Simplify and verify by substituting back
**Check**: Verify both roots satisfy the original equation

## Template: Solving Systems of Equations (Substitution)
**Step 1**: Solve one equation for one variable
**Step 2**: Substitute into the other equation
**Step 3**: Solve the resulting single-variable equation
**Step 4**: Back-substitute to find the other variable
**Step 5**: Verify the solution in both original equations

## Template: Solving Inequalities
**Step 1**: Isolate the variable expression
**Step 2**: Find critical points (where expression = 0 or undefined)
**Step 3**: Test intervals between critical points using a sign chart
**Step 4**: Write the solution set using interval notation
**Note**: Remember to flip the inequality sign when multiplying/dividing by a negative number

## Template: Partial Fractions
**Step 1**: Ensure the degree of numerator < degree of denominator (else do polynomial division first)
**Step 2**: Factor the denominator completely
**Step 3**: Write the partial fraction form based on factor types
**Step 4**: Multiply both sides by the denominator
**Step 5**: Solve for the unknown coefficients using convenient values of x or by comparing coefficients

## Template: Sequence and Series Problems
**Step 1**: Identify the type (AP, GP, or other)
**Step 2**: List known quantities (first term, common difference/ratio, number of terms)
**Step 3**: Apply the appropriate formula (nth term, sum formula)
**Step 4**: Solve for the unknown
**Step 5**: Verify with a few terms
